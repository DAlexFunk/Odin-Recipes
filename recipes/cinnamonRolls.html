Cinnamon Rolls

Description

This delicious cinnamon roll recipe is like none other. What sets it apart? No. Yeast. This cuts down on the time needed to make the recipe and is great for beginner bakers.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoone baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 deg F. Brush an 8 in. square baking dish with 1 tablespoon melted butter.
  2. Make dough: Whisk flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Work 3 tablespoons softened butter into the flour mix until butter is lentil sized.
  3. Pour milk into flour-butter mix and stir with spatula until a soft dough forms.
  4. Turn dough out onto a well-floured work surface and roll into a 10 in. by 10 in. square. Brush surface with remaining 2 tablespoons melted butter.
  5. To make the filling: Whisk Â¼ cup white sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon cinnamon-sugar mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon sugar over butter-brushed dough.
  6. Roll dough around filling to form a log; cut log into 9 rolls about 1-inch wide; place rolls, cut-side up, in the prepared baking dish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until rolls are puffed and lightly golden, 20 to 25 minutes.
  8. To make the frosting: Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, 1/4 cup softened butter, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until frosting is smooth.
  9. Top hot cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting and enjoy!

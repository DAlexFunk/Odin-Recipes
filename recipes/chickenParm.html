Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is one of the most popular dishes from Italy (other than pasta of course). While most of the time, you may get it from Olive Garden, it is actualy a pretty simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of panko bread crumbs (can add more if needed)
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 table spoon of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of mozzarella cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup of grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 deg F
  2. Place chickn breasts between twi sheets of heavy plastic and firmly pound to a thickness of 1/2 in.
  3. Season chicken thoroughly with salt and pepper. Sprinkle flour over each breast with an even coat
  4. Beat eggs into a shallow bowl
  5. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese in a separate bowl
  6. Dip flour-coated chicken breast in the beaten eggs. Transfer the breat to bread crumb mixture pressing into both sides. Repeat for each breast. Let sit for 10 to 15 minutes.
  7. Heat 1/2 inch olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high until it begins to simmer. Cook chicken in the oil until golden.
  8. Transfer chicken to baking dish. Top each breast with 2 tabelspoons of tomato sauce. Layer each breast with equal amounts of mozzarella cheese , fresh basil, and provolone cheese. Sprinkle remaining parmesan cheese over top and drizzle with 1/2 teaspoon of olive oil
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is brown and bubbly and chicken breasts are not longer pink in center and enjoy!
